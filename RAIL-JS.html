1. Version Rationale: Science vs. Simulation

Standard generative AI models often prioritize "fluency" over "accuracy". In scientific disciplines like toxicology and pharmacology, a fluent hallucination can be dangerous. RAIL is built on the principle of Objective Realism. We assume the model's internal training data is at least 18 months old and prone to creative drift.

RAIL addresses this by turning the LLM from a "speaker" into an "analytical engine". By providing a tether (the Knowledge Base and Document Context), we force the model to look at the world as it is presented to it, rather than how it remembers it. This version focuses on individualised RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), allowing you to curate your own private specialized knowledge sets that overwrite the AI's standard biases.

2. API Keys, Connectivity & Commercial AI Services

RAIL is a "bring-your-own-intelligence" platform. It provides the advanced scaffolding, but you provide the engine and the fuel. This ensures your private research data isn't leaked into generic model training loops.

Exact Procedure to Connect to Commercial AI Services:

  1. Open the System Registry panel by clicking the ⚙️ Settings button in the left navigation sidebar.
  2. Toggle the protocol selector dropdown to HTTPS (mandatory for all commercial cloud services to comply with secure browser origin policies).
  3. In the RAIL Service Endpoint input field, enter the custom endpoint path matching your provider (see the matrix below). Do not enter the https:// prefix itself, as it is automatically prefixed by the toggle.
  4. Enter your commercial API key into the API Key (Optional) field.
  5. Enter the exact Model Name identifier in the Model Name input field.
  6. Click Save Registry Configuration. Your settings will persist in your local session.

3. Dual-Agent Orchestration Strategy

The central innovation of RAIL is the split between Extraction and Synthesis.

CLERK (LITERALIST)

Zero inferences. It sees text as symbols to be mapped. Use this for dose responses, chemical IDs, and quoting methods.

ARCHITECT (STRATEGIST)

Holistic logic. Connects extraction to clinical implications. Use this for risk assessment and summary conclusions.

4. Cognitive Augmentation Settings

Fine-tune the "brain" of RAIL with toggleable heuristics:

  • Devil's Advocate: Prevents "yes-man" AI behavior. It will aggressively look for p-hacking, small sample sizes, and logical gaps in the data provided.
  • Multi-Perspective Evaluation: Shifts the AI through different scientist personas. A "Statisician" will look at the numbers, while a "Clinical Toxicologist" looks at patient outcomes.
  • Epistemic Confidence: Forces the AI to state "I don't know" when information is missing, rather than generating "most likely" scenarios.
  • Causal Reasoning: Focuses specifically on mechanistic pathways (A → B → C) which is critical for understanding toxicity mechanisms.

5. Tethered Mind & Logic Auditing

RAIL uses Computational Safeguards to ensure the model doesn't drift.

  • Semantic Drift Protection: The AI checks its own answers against the original abstract/summary. If it starts to wander into generic AI tropes, the audit log will flag a "Context Dissociation" event.
  • Logic Auditor: Performs a secondary pass on any mathematical claims. If the model claims 10/20 is 5%, the Auditor will block the output.
  • Zero-Shot Prohibition: The ultimate safety. If the facts aren't in your document or KB, the AI is physically forbidden from answering.

6. The Multi-Modal Data Visualizer

RAIL is capable of "Self-Correcting Graphics". When complex data is processed, the AI can generate Charts (Chart.js) or Flowcharts (Mermaid.js). The right-side Data Viewer provides a sandbox where these visual artifacts are rendered instantly. You can toggle this panel to keep the focus on the discussion or zoom into the extracted patterns.

7. The Audit Trace: Total Oversight

Every message you send is wrapped in a "System Instruction Layer" that enforces your chosen constraints. The Audit log exposes these invisible layers. This allows scientists to verify which heuristics were active during a specific extraction, fulfilling modern requirement for Explainable AI (XAI) in research.

8. Personal Knowledge Base & RAG Index

This is the backbone of RAIL's specialized intelligence. You can curate a private library of verified facts that RAIL prioritizes above all else.

📝 Crucial workflow: Verification and Entry Editing Every single knowledge base entry is fully editable. When you search NCBI PubMed or the Web via the Knowledge Miner, clicking on any draft search result opens the Source Verification & Preview window.

How to expand and enrich studies:
  1. Click the Open Original Link button (or click the URL path) inside the verification window. This opens the full research paper or abstract in an external browser window.
  2. Inspect the paper's full text, including dosage tables, experimental models, or exact p-values.
  3. Directly copy and paste these detailed findings from the external window into the Content/Snippet Textarea of the Source Verification window. You can append or overwrite the default draft abstract.
  4. Modify the Title or Category if needed, then click Verify & Save to KB.

Once saved, this enriched data is locked into your RAG engine. When the Dual-Agent Orchestration runs, the AI will pull from this precise verified text instead of generic memories.
  • Database Engines: RAIL supports dual-mode storage. LocalStorage is ideal for standalone application use (private, fast, no cloud connectivity required). Firebase Cloud is designed for authorized server-based implementations where research datasets need to be synchronized across devices. Note that standalone applications are not natively designed to interface with Firebase; connecting them may require additional manual configuration. For full cloud integration, the online hosted version of RAIL is recommended.
  • Knowledge Miner: Search the web for specific papers. Use the (PM) prefix (e.g., (PM) glyphosate toxicity) to connect directly to NCBI PubMed for authoritative abstracts without needing a web search key.
  • Selective Indexing: Check the checkboxes for data points you trust before committing them to your Index.
  • Register-Style Retrieval: Instead of dumping everything into the context (which confuses AI), RAIL "registers" your entries. For every query, it selectively fetches the 4 most relevant entries from your database, ensuring high-fidelity recall without context bloat.
  • Precedence Protocol: Your verified data always wins. If the AI "thinks" a chemical is toxic but your KB entry says it isn't, the AI will use your data points.

9. Advanced Research Intelligence Features

RAIL includes specialized tools for large-scale research management and verifiable citation tracking.

  • Active Citation Linking: When the AI references a study using the (PM ID: 12345) or (PM 12345) format, RAIL automatically converts these into interactive links. Clicking a link will check if the source is in your local Knowledge Base; if so, it opens the internal preview. If not, it safely directs you to the official PubMed record.
  • Metadata Categorization: Every entry in your Training Index can be tagged (e.g., "Toxicology", "Pharmacology", "Regulatory"). This allows for future granular filtering and ensures your datasets remain organized even as they grow into the hundreds.
  • Intelligence Synchronization: RAIL simplifies moving from solo research to a collaborative environment. Use the "Sync Offline Data to Cloud" feature to instantly migrate local findings to your shared Firebase index while automatically filtering out duplicates.
  • Research Dashboard: The Knowledge Base Manager includes a high-level health check, tracking total entries, source diversity, and the timestamp of your last intelligence audit.
  • Batch Lifecycle Management: Use the multi-select checkboxes in the Training Index to perform bulk operations, such as exporting a subset of findings for a specific project report or purging outdated data.
  • Portable Intelligence (JSON): You can export your entire Training Index to a localized JSON file at any time. This allows you to back up your findings or move your specialized knowledge from a local instance to a cloud-tethered implemention without data loss.
